C2S

Converts cell to structure array.


 Synopsis:
  out = c2s({'item1',value1,'item2',value2,...})

 Description:
  out = c2s({'item1',value1,'item2',value2,...}) is a shortcut 
   for out = struct('item1',value1,'item2',value2,...).

 Example:
  out = c2s({'a',1,'b',2,'c','Hello'})

Source: c2s.m
