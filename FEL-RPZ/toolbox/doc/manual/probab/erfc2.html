ERFC2

Normal cumulative distribution function.


 Synopsis:
  y=erfc2(x,m,v)
 
 Description:
  It computes normal cumulative distribution function 

  erfc2(x) = 1/sqrt(2*pi)*v) * 
    integral from x to inf of exp(-(t-m)^2/(2*v^2) dt

 Input:
  x [1x1] Real number.
  m [1x1] Mean value.
  v [1x1] Variance.

 Output:
  y [1x1] Error function.

 See also
  ERFC.
